About Us

There are various student counselling and guidance systems present for purposes like for example, Early Warning System to identify at-risk students and preventing dropping out. An intelligent tutoring system, helping students to study and learn the concepts in the course by giving navigational support according to their knowledge level. Career counselling system is used to predict the suitable department for an individual based on their skills assessed by an objective test and previous historical academics oriented records. A good college or university recommendation system may help students to plan their future accordingly & create their own opportunities which could lead to a better life.

The main idea of this project is to build a ML/DL based model that makes prediction for the possibility of entering a specific college, programme, branch or stream specialization based on HSE scores procured & a ranked list of colleges giving a state of peace of mind to students & parents.
